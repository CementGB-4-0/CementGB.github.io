About Cement

Cement is a mod loader built for Gang Beasts using BepInEx, written in the C# Language, and is what holds users' mods together.

Cement provides helpful modding tools, making it easy for creators to make mods, and users to play them.

The Cement team is a small group of passionate people who love Gang Beasts. If you like what we are doing, please consider donating to help support us and the future development of mods and modding tools for Gang Beasts.
