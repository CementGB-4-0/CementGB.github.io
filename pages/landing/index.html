About Cement

Cement requires MelonLoader.
Cement is a modding utility built for Gang Beasts made with MelonLoader, written in the C# programming language. It is what holds most users' mods together.

Cement provides helpful modding tools, making it easy for creators to make Gang Beasts mods (and users to play them), without reinventing the wheel.
